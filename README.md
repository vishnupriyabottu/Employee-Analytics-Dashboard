**Employee Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)**

## Project Overview
This project analyzes employee data to deliver actionable insights into workforce distribution, salary and bonus structures, hiring trends, and senior management cost impact.  
The dashboard is designed to support HR teams and leadership in data-driven decision making.

---

##  Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- CSV(Data Source)

** Key Insights**
- Senior management contributes **47% of total salary cost**
- Salary distribution varies significantly across departments
- Hiring peaked during specific growth years
- Certain teams have higher senior concentration impacting overall cost
